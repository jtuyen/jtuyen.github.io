John started his extensive career as an IT professional and transitioned into IT security field as a cybersecurity consultant for public and private sectors across North America. He embodies the entrepreneurial spirit and a hacker's mentality which helped accelerate his career path. His proven technical skills as a penetration tester and foundational knowledge of governance, risk and compliance illuminate his well-rounded capabilities. He's an advocate of the holistic approach to information security and it's protection against cyber attacks isn't only about technology, it includes people and process. Within his connected network, he's known as the cheerful go-to guy when able to be at service for others. A community minded individual with a sense of agency to establish long-term relationships, trust, and confidence with those who require his expertise and humility.
